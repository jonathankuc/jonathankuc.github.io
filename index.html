Carbon Footprint Calculator

The Carbon Footprint Calculator app and website are one of the first projects that I dedicated a considerable amount of time and effort into during high school. The goal of the app was to help users calculate their carbon footprint based on a few different factors such as their electricity, natural gas, and heating oil bills. The website was created to help with marketing and informing the users about the app. My primary focus was on designing the overall UI of the app and elements of the website.

This project was developed as part of a school-wide science fair and our group was awarded the ‘Most Likely to Succeed in the Marketplace’ and ‘Third Place in the Design Category’ awards at WSU’s Imagine Tomorrow Competition in 2013.

Praxis Early Childhood Education

The Praxis Early Childhood Education website was an opportunity in which I worked independently with a local non-profit organization in the Seattle area to help them establish a web presence. The main objective of the site was to give the organization a way to distribute important information to their supporters in a centralized location, and to also showcase some of the work they have been doing. Working with the head of the organization, we strived to create an aesthetically pleasing interface while maintaining a simple design to ensure that all important information was easily accessible by visitors to the site.
